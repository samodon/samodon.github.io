3D Artwork

Project Date March 9th, 2024
Languages Swift, Python, Objective C
Technologies CNN, Machine Learning, Synthetic Data
Visit github.com/samodon/WorkOutUpdatedUI

Cyber Gym solves my problem of staying motivated during workouts by developing an AI-powered workout monitoring app using SwiftUI and Core ML. This app leverages computer vision algorithms to accurately detect exercises like sit-ups and push-ups and is designed to expand to support a wider range of workouts. Key features include real-time competitions and a leaderboard system, allowing users to challenge themselves and their friends in daily workout battles while tracking their progress and climbing the ranks. The competitive element fosters motivation and encourages continuous improvement, making fitness routines more engaging and enjoyable.

To enhance the app's accuracy and user experience, I implemented synthetic data generation using Blender, significantly boosting the AI model's performance and flexibility. Additionally, I incorporated Adobe Mixamo animations to create a smooth and immersive user experience. This innovative approach not only ensures precise exercise detection and real-time feedback but also gamifies the workout process, keeping users motivated and committed to their fitness goals. The combination of advanced technology and gamification effectively transforms traditional workouts into a fun and competitive experience.
